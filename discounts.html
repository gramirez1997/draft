Discounted Pet Services in San Antonio

Adoption Is Easy

Pet adoption can seem overwhelming at first but is actually fairly easy. The first step is finding an adoption center near you. Once you locate a center you like, no appointment is needed. You can simply walk right in. In almost all centers you can sit/walk with the pet you are interested in so you can both get familiar with each other. Once you have found a pet just let a staff member know. They will direct you to fill out some paperwork with basic questions about you that mostly focus on your home.
TIP! Most centers have adoption days or promotions that cut the adoption fee in half, just ask or call to find out!

PetsSmart

Find the closet PetsSmart

Many people don't know that when you adopt a pet from your humane society or pound you can recieve an adoption booklet that contains free products and coupons from PetsSmart. All you have to do is bring in your adoption paperwork to the cashier register and they will hand you the booklet.
