Meet MaMa Our Featured Pet of The Month

Our Featured Pet is a terrier pix mixed name Mama! She was returned to Animal care services twice, Sadly this last return would have been the end of the road for her. Fortunately she was rescued and placed into a foster home. However this is a temporary home and is still looking for a forever home! Mama is very sweet and loving. She would do best in a home with no other pets. She is very energetic and would be great match to someone who loves to go on hikes and trails. Contact me for more infromation!

Success Stories

Our latest success story comes from a strong-willed pup named Bigboy. He was found wandering the street with severe mange and double hip dysplasia. Bigboy had almost given up hope when he was rescued and placed into a forever home. After some time, care, and love he is a brand-new pup.

Not all animals are rescued from the animal shelter or humane society. Gabriel the kitty was found lurking outside a woman's house begging for food and a warm home. He found his way into her heart and into her home! He now lives inside and cuddles with his fellow rescued brothers.

This cute pup is named Sophie! Her smile never wavered as she waited over 2 years in Odessa humane society for a forever home! She is currently living a carefree life and enjoying all the doggie treats.
