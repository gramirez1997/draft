About Me

Hello, my name is Gabriella Ramirez, I was born and raised in San Antonio. This site Is created is to encourage San Antonio residents to go and adopt from a shelter near you! Ever since I can remember I have always been surrounded by all kinds of animals. As a family we never turned away a stray in need. We have always fostered dogs and cats, helping find them a forever home. As an owner of 7 cats, 5 dogs, and 2 fosters, I understand pet ownership can be expensive. With the creation of this site, I hope to inspire San Antonio residents to investigate the programs listed and realize pet ownership does not have to be costly.
